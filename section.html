We all know our doggie friends love juicy meat dishes, and as a treat this is more than suitable as a good treat. Just remember, lean meats generally mean chicken, fish and red meat. All of this should be unseasoned, without any sauces or spices, and cut into manageable pieces as usual. Always avoid processed meats like bacon, sausage, chorizo etc. No matter how much they whine!
